Mission

The website is intend to increase revenue for FRIAS, a Kids Wear fashion outlet, by providing online shopping. And also attract new customers go shopping at outlet.

The number of how many people browse the website and the number of how many deal making on the website are two important symbol of website success.

Feedback form is another way to evaluate the website succeess based on the customers' feedback.

Target Audience

The woman who have the 2-10 years old children and want to buy children's product online , almost very busy and no time to go to shopping centre.
