CP1406 - Assignment 2 - FRIAS (Kids fashion store)

Group2

Mission

The website is intended to make FRIAS, a Kids Wear fashion outlet, popular by providing customers with the entire range of clothing options available at FRIAS.Website intends to provide a simple and easy interface for people to place orders and make payments. Website also intends to promote and increase the number of patrons at the cafe and fashion shows that are going to take place in the near future.

 

Success Evaluation

The number of people who attend FRIAS events and make purchases at FRIAS store is expected to increase by atleast 30%. By making people aware of the products available at FRIAS more people are likely to place orders and purchase clothes.

Target Audience

The website is targeted at young children and parents who are interested in looking for and shopping for clothes on the internet.
